Privacy Policy

Ember · Shapestone LLC
Effective date: June 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Ember (the “App”), provided by Shapestone LLC (“we,” “us”), handles your information. In short: Ember is built to work entirely on your device, and we do not collect your personal information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal information on our servers. Ember has no user accounts and requires no sign-in. Everything you create and do in the App stays on your device.

Data Stored on Your Device

The following is stored locally on your device and is not sent to us:

  • Your decks, cards, and any content you create or import
  • Your study history, scheduling data, and progress
  • Your app settings and preferences
  • A local identifier kept in your device’s keychain, used only to manage your in-app purchase on that device. It is not transmitted to us.

This data remains on your device and is removed when you delete the App.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment details. Apple’s handling of your purchase is governed by Apple’s own privacy policy.

Third-Party Services and Tracking

Ember does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking, and does not share your data with third parties. We do not use cookies or similar technologies.

Children’s Privacy

Ember is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the App does not collect personal information from anyone, this applies to all users.

Data Security

Because your data stays on your device, its security is protected by your device’s own safeguards, such as your passcode and device encryption.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date above and post the updated policy on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at emberfeedback@shapestone.com.
