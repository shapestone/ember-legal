Frequently asked questions

How do I restore a purchase?

If you’ve bought Ember Lifetime and it isn’t showing up — for example on a new device — open the upgrade screen and tap Restore Purchases. Your purchase is tied to your Apple ID, so it restores on any device signed in with the same account.

How does Ember decide when to show a card?

Ember uses spaced repetition (the FSRS algorithm): it surfaces each card right before you’d be likely to forget it, so every review lands at the most effective moment. The more you review, the better it tunes the timing to you.

Where is my data, and is it private?

Everything stays on your device — Ember has no account and collects no personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details. Because your data is stored locally, deleting the app also removes your decks and study history, so keep that in mind before uninstalling.

How do I report a bug or request a feature?

Email emberfeedback@shapestone.com with as much detail as you can. For bugs, it helps to include what you did, what happened, and your device model and iOS version. We genuinely read and act on this feedback.
